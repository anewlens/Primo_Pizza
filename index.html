Who We Are

Local

At Primo Pizza, we offer great food at a great price. From our pizza to our subs to our pasta, we are passionate about what we do. We use only the freshest ingredients and offer dine in, carryout, and delivery. We are the best choice for pizza delivery in Mechanicsville, VA.

Quality

As one of the premiere delivery restaurants in Mechanicsville, we want to serve our local community with quality food at a quick serve pace. We use fresh ingredients, and even source locally produced food items when in season. We live here too and we want to help keep our local economy strong and prosperous. Think local, buy local, and be a good neighbor.

Convenient

To best serve our community, we offer dine-in, delivery and take-out, so whether you're throwing a party, heading out to eat, or picking up dinner, you can count on our great taste and value.
